# Taipower Substation Room & Indoor Wiring Review Documents

## Context & Purpose
This skill provides the exact checklist of documents required by the Taiwan Power Company (Taipower) for the design review of outdoor/indoor electrical substation rooms (配電場所圖審) and indoor wiring systems (屋內線路圖審) according to Taipower Operating Rules Articles 42 and 44.

## Document Checklists

### 1. Outdoor Substation Room (屋外配電場所)
| Item | Required Document | Quantity |
|------|-------------------|----------|
| 1 | Original Building Permit (建造執照正本) | 1 |
| 2 | Copy of Building Permit (建造執照影本) | 1 |
| 3 | Copy of Architectural Drawings (建築圖副本) | 1 |
| 4 | Cadastral Site Plan (地籍配置圖表) | 1 |
| 5 | 1st Floor Plan (一樓平面圖) | 4 |
| 6 | Letter of Commitment (承諾書) | 1 |

### 2. Indoor Substation Room (屋內配電場所)
| Item | Required Document | Quantity |
|------|-------------------|----------|
| 1 | Original Building Permit (建造執照正本) | 1 |
| 2 | Copy of Building Permit (建造執照影本) | 1 |
| 3 | Copy of Architectural Drawings (建築圖副本) | 1 |
| 4 | Cadastral Site Plan (地籍配置圖表) | 1 |
| 5 | Substation Room & Shaft Floor Plan (設置配電場所含管道間樓層平面圖) | 4 |
| 6 | 1st Floor Plan (一樓平面圖) | 1 |
| 7 | Substation Room Section Drawing (配電場所室剖面圖) | 1 |
| 8 | Structural Drawing of Ceiling above Substation Room (配電場所上層結構圖) | 1 |
| 9 | Waterproof Gate Design (For basement rooms) (防水閘門板設計圖) | 1 |
| 10| Letter of Commitment (承諾書) | 1 |

### 3. Indoor Wiring Review (屋內線路圖審)
| Item | Required Document | Quantity |
|------|-------------------|----------|
| 1 | Copy of Building or Use Permit (建築執照或使用執照影本) | 1 |
| 2 | Project Outline (設戶工程概要) | 1 |
| 3 | Cadastral Map (地籍位置圖) | 1 |
| 4 | Single-Line Diagram (單線系統圖) | 1 |
| 5 | Load Schedule (負載表) | 1 |

## Additional Requirements
If the electrical power design review meets the conditions of the "Criteria for Determining the Scope of Engineering Design and Supervision of Electrical Utility Equipment and Customer Electrical Equipment", it MUST be designed by a legally registered Professional Electrical Engineer (電機技師) or related professional engineer.

## Execution Logic for AI
When reviewing a submission package for Taipower:
1. Identify if the substation is indoor or outdoor.
2. Cross-reference the provided document list against the appropriate checklist above.
3. Flag any missing quantities (e.g., needing 4 copies of the 1st floor plan for outdoor, or 4 copies of the substation floor plan for indoor).
4. For basement indoor substations, ensure a waterproof gate design drawing is included.
