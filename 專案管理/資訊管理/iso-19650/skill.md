# ISO 19650 - BIM Information Management

## Overview
ISO 19650 是建築資訊管理（BIM）的國際標準系列，提供建築資產全生命週期的資訊管理框架。目前有效版本為2018-2025各Part，2026年預計更新DIS版本。

## Standard Parts

| Part | Title | Year | Status |
|------|-------|------|-------|
| ISO 19650-1 | Concepts and Principles | 2018 | Active |
| ISO 19650-2 | Delivery Phase of Assets | 2018 | Under Revision |
| ISO 19650-3 | Operational Phase of Assets | 2020 | Under Revision |
| ISO 19650-4 | Information Exchange | 2022 | Active |
| ISO 19650-5 | Security-minded Approach | 2020 | Active |
| ISO 19650-6 | Health and Safety Information | 2025 | Active |

## Core Concepts

### Information Management Process (9 Steps)
1. **Assessment & Need** - 評估與需求
2. **Invitation to Tender** - 招標
3. **Tender Response** - 投標回應
4. **Appointment** - 任命
5. **Mobilisation** - 動員
6. **Information Production** - 資訊產製
7. **Information Exchange** - 資訊交換
8. **Information Model** - 資訊模型
9. **Closeout** - 結案

### Key Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|----------|
| AIM | Asset Information Model |

| CDE | Common Data Environment |

| AIR | Appointing Party's Information Requirements |
| LOD | Level of Development → LOIN (2026) |
| PIM | Project Information Model |

## 2018 vs 2026 Key Differences

| 2018 Version | 2026 Revision (DIS) |
|---------------|---------------------|
| BIM | IM (Information Management) |
| BEP | IPP (Information Production Plan) |
| LOD | LOIN (Level of Information Need) |
| Separate delivery/operational phases | Unified 9-step process |
| Model-centric | Information-centric |
